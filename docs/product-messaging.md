## Target Audiences

### Primary: Small-to-mid-size staffing firms (5–50 employees)
- **Pain**: Using spreadsheets, email, and 3+ disconnected tools
- **Goal**: One unified system that's fast to adopt and doesn't require a consultant
- **Message**: "Up and running in minutes, not months."

### Secondary: Trade & HVAC recruiters
- **Pain**: Generic ATS tools don't handle hourly/contract/trade workflows well
- **Goal**: Track skilled trades candidates, manage contractor placements, handle both hourly and salary
- **Message**: "Purpose-built for hands-on industries."

### Tertiary: Internal recruiting / HR teams (10–200 person companies)
- **Pain**: No ATS or using an overly complex enterprise system
- **Goal**: Lightweight candidate tracking with hiring manager visibility
- **Message**: "Give your hiring managers a portal, not a spreadsheet."

## Use-Case Positioning

### 1. Internal Staffing Firm OS
**Positioning**: Bedrock is the operating system for your staffing agency. Manage candidates, jobs, clients, placements, tasks, and revenue from one system.

**Key messages**:
- "One login for your entire operation"
- "See pipeline-to-placement in real time"
- "Track recruiter activity and revenue by client"

### 2. HVAC / Trade Recruiting Platform
**Positioning**: The first recruiting platform designed for skilled trades. Handle hourly rates, contract placements, and field-ready candidate profiles.

**Key messages**:
- "Built for industries that build things"
- "Hourly, salary, contract — all in one system"
- "Your candidates have skills, not just resumes"

### 3. Recruiting Workflow Software
**Positioning**: Bedrock replaces your spreadsheets, email threads, and sticky notes with a clean workflow: source → screen → submit → place.

**Key messages**:
- "From first contact to placement — one workflow"
- "No training required — if you can use email, you can use Bedrock"
- "Automation that actually saves time"
